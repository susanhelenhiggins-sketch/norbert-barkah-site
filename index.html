Meet Norbert

A gentle soul with a torn ear, a wise heart, and a way of making people feel at home

Norbert Barkah is the heart of this world. He is a gentle, intuitive dog with expressive eyes, a soft spirit, and a torn ear that feels like part of his story, too. People may first notice how handsome he is, but what stays with them is his presence. He listens. He feels. He seems to understand when a moment needs tenderness.

What began as reading stories to Norbert became something much bigger. His quiet attention helped open the door to storytime videos, enchanted forest tales, and a growing world of books and healing stories. He is not just the inspiration behind the work, he is part of the magic people come here to feel.

Children's Picture Book

The Tug of War Lesson

The Tug of War Lesson is the story that started it all. When I first shared it as a storytime video with Norbert, viewers immediately began asking where they could buy the book. That response showed me this story had touched something real, and it became the spark that helped bring this work into the world in book form.

At its heart, this is a gentle story about emotional wisdom, kindness, and choosing peace over struggle. Told with warmth and simple truth, it invites children and the grown-ups who read with them to feel steadier, safer, and more connected to their own inner light.

Enter the Enchanted Forest

A story world rooted in kindness, courage, healing, and quiet light

The enchanted forest is where Norbert's deeper stories live. It is a place of glowing paths, gentle creatures, listening trees, and moments that help children and adults make sense of big feelings in soft, meaningful ways. These stories are written with heart, but they also carry deeper truths about grief, courage, friendship, and becoming who we really are.

🌿

Kindness

In this forest, kindness is never small. A soft choice, a patient heart, or a gentle word can change the whole path of a story.

✨

Courage

Courage here does not shout. It shows up in tender steps, honest feelings, and the quiet strength to keep going when the way feels uncertain.

🌞

Inner Light

Every story holds a lantern. These tales help readers remember that even in fear, grief, or confusion, there is still light within to guide the next step.

About Mama Sue

Stories written with heart, shared to bring comfort, courage, and connection

I'm Susan Higgins, a storyteller, artist, and heart guide who believes in the power of kindness, imagination, and inner light. My stories, shared with Norbert Barkah by my side, are created to help children and grown-ups find courage, connection, and calm in uncertain times. Inspired by the magic of the natural world and the quiet strength of healing, I write to offer a softer place to land.

What began as reading stories to Norbert grew into something much bigger. People did not just listen, they felt something. From those simple storytime moments came a book, a growing enchanted forest world, and a deeper calling to create stories that comfort, encourage, and help people remember the light within themselves.
